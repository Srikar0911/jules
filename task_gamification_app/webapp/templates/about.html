About TaskGamify

Welcome to TaskGamify, the application designed to make your to-do list more engaging and fun!

Our mission is to blend productivity with play. We believe that by adding elements of gamification—like points for completing tasks and a competitive leaderboard—we can motivate you to stay on top of your goals, whether they are for work, study, or personal projects.

How It Works

  • Create Tasks: Add the things you need to get done.
  • Earn Points: Complete your tasks to earn points.
  • Climb the Leaderboard: See how you stack up against other users. A little friendly competition can be a great motivator.
  • Stay Organized: Keep all your tasks in one place, and track your progress over time.

This project is built with Python, Flask, and SQLAlchemy, demonstrating a clean separation between the core application logic and the web interface.

Ready to get started? Sign up now and turn your tasks into triumphs!
